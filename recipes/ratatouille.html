Ratatouille

Wanna some health and delicious food? well this is the right choice.

Ratatouille might not have the better look but for sure is a good option for a family meal at sundays.

Ingredients

  • 2 Zucchinis
  • 2 Eggplants
  • 2 Onion
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • Olive oil to taste
  • Rosemary to taste
  • Basil to taste
  • Garlic to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Bay leaf to taste
  • Tomato sauce

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Directions

  1. Slice the vegetables
  2. Cover the bottom of the pan with tomato sause.
  3. Assemble everything interspersing with vegetables.
  4. Smash the garlic and spread over, adding salt and rosemary to taste.
  5. Drizzle with a little olive oil and bake with baking paper or aluminum.
  6. Take to the oven for 40 minutes at a temperature of 180° C to 200° C.
